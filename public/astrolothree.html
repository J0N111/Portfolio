ASTROLOTHREE

 
Previous Next

Astrolothree is a wave based top-down fighting game wherein the player character has three weapons that slay enemies with varying effectiveness. My contribution to the project is in the form of all the sprites and buttons featured throughout the game and menus. As this was my first time animating sprites as well as weapons and attacks, the time constraint of a week paired with the jam’s theme of three played a large part in both the overall scope of the sprites as well as their look.

Because of the time crunch, I used the art application I was most familiar with – Procreate – to make all of the sprites. In hindsight, it would have been much more effective to use an art program better equipped to make pixel art. There were many times where I had to go over sections of the sprites multiple times because the brush opacity would vary depending on how long the brush stroke was. I failed to think about how much wasted time this would add up to as I drew and colored each character. To add to the frustration, anytime I moved the character using the selection tool there was a high chance that part of the sprite would stick to the page leaving a ghost of the sprite while also making a few of the pixels slightly transparent.

Although I struggled to meet the demands of the game with the program I used, I was able to draw and animate over 100 sprites for the game! I learned that keeping certain sections of the sprite black-and-white would allow for the use of colored glow effects without the need of an emission map. If I were to redo this project, I would definitely use a dedicated pixel art program and play around with the height and shadows of my characters more.
