BOXCAR

 

This was the product of a semester long project that walked us through the process of modeling and implementing our boxcar into a driving game of our own creation. My biggest inspiration for this project was Tron: legacy and this influence can be seen throughout the modeling and texturing of the vehicle.

 

My first go at modeling the boxcar fit the given soapbox car images perfectly before I decided to go all in and model, texture and animate a car that would fit Tronâ€™s asthetic. The biggest problem I had when modeling was getting the doors to open and not clip through or tear itself apart. I ended up having to redo a large portion of both halves of the car to make the vertices match up better on the inside and outside. Aside from tweaking the vertices, the rest of the modeling and animating went fairly smoothly. The only other hurdle I faced was the over 10 hour rendering session my computer went through for the creation of the intro scene for the game.

 

I still think of the model fondly although I believe that the intro video and game assets could have been better. If I were to redo this project, I would make scenery models in Maya to accompany the driving track and also try to incorporate better UI throughout the game.
